Discover Your New Best Friend

  • Scope: Research & User Testing, Wireframes, Interactive Prototype
  • Tools: Proto.IO, Pen & Paper, Markers & Sharpies
  • Duration: 5 Days

Prototype in Action

The Project

Working with a team of developers, conceptualize, design and build an interactive tool integrated with a database.

The Problem

Too many animals are not getting adopted from rescue shelters due to a variety of reasons. We see an opportunity to break down the barriers in the adoption process and bring ease and joy of finding a pet that matches your personal needs.

Our Solution

A fun and engaging mobile app that allows you to discover local rescue pets based on your personal pet preferences.
